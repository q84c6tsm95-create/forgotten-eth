About Forgotten ETH

What is this?

A tool to find and recover ETH stuck in defunct smart contracts on Ethereum. Thousands of ETH remain locked in old DEXes, NFT marketplaces, ENS auctions, and ICO contracts that shut down years ago. Portfolio trackers do not detect these balances.

How it works

Connect your wallet or search any address to check for unclaimed ETH across 22 contracts. All withdrawals go directly to your wallet from the original smart contract. If a balance is found, the site crafts a withdrawal transaction for you to review and sign. You can also claim directly from Etherscan.
